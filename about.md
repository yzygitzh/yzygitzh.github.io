Ziyue Yang is a senior researcher in [Networking Infrastructure Group](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/networking-research-group-2/), Microsoft Research Asia.

He received his MS degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2020, advised by Professor [Yao Guo](https://yaoguopku.github.io). He received his BS degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2017.

He currently focuses on Cloud and AI Infrastructure. Previously, he also worked on Mobile System and Mobile App Analysis.

His publications can be found at [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=MdoxXlsAAAAJ).
